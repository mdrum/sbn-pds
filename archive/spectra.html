Spectra and Spectrophotometry

Spectrophotometry is the quantitative measurement of the reflection or transmission properties of a material as a function of wavelength.
This data can also be commonly represented as a chart or a graph that shows the intensity of light being emitted over a range of wavelengths (or wavenumbers).
