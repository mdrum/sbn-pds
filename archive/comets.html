Data Sets Containing Comet Data

  • Fink Images and Spectra of Solar System Objects
  • NEOWISE Diameters and Albedos (contains several comets with Centaur-like orbits)
  • MSX Small Bodies Images (contains infrared images of eight comets from the MSX mission)
  • NEAR MSI - acquired visual images of comet C/Hyakutake on March 23, 1996. Look for data from mission-elased times from 3089211 to 3092473.
  • Stooke Shape Models (contains a shape model of Comet Halley)
  • TNO and Centaur Colors (contains broadband colors of several comets with Centaur-like orbits)

For more PDS Comet data, see the PDS Small Bodies Node Comet Archive Site .
