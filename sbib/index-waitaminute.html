Small Bodies Image Browser

SBIB is a web application for searching available images of various orbited small bodies, visually. SBIB allows you to find images by coordinate boxes, mission parameters, resolution, and others, then preview and download them in FITS, PNG, or JPEG.

New targets are being added regularly, and we also have a new 3D version of this application in development!

Currently supported targets:

  • OSIRIS-REx Mission to Bennu
  • New Horizons Encounter with Pluto
  • Dawn Mission to Ceres
  • Dawn Mission to Vesta
  • NEAR Shoemaker Mission to 433 Eros
  • Cassini Mission: Rhea (Saturn V)
  • Cassini Mission: Iapetus (Saturn VIII)
  • Cassini Mission: Enceladus (Saturn II)
Open SBIB
