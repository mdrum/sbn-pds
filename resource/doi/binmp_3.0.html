Overview

  • Abstract: This bundle contains orbital and physical properties for well-observed or suspected binary/multiple minor planets including the Pluto system, compiled from the published literature. In total 370 companions in 351 systems are included. This data set is complete for binary/multiple components reported through 31 March 2019.
