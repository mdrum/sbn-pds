Overview

  • Abstract: This bundle contains all reported timings of observed asteroid, planet, and planetary satellite occultation events, as well as axes derived from those timings by David W. Dunham and David Herald. Most of these timings are unpublished and have been collected by Dunham and Herald. This version is complete through the end of 2016.
