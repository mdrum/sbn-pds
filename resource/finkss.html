Fink Images and Spectra of Solar System Objects

Status: Certified and released with minor liens, April 27, 2017

This data set contains raw images and spectra of predominantly solar system objects, standard stars, and calibration frames acquired by Uwe Fink and collaborators on Mt. Bigelow near Tucson AZ from 1984 to 1998. Also provided are the scanned handwritten observing logs and the corresponding generated (reviewed) logs with the corrected information. A table with the filter information and if available filter curves are also included.
