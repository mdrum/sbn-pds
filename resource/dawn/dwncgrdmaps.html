Dawn GRaND Ceres Maps

Status: Archived

This data set contains the Dawn Ceres high level map products including Water Equivalent Hydrogen (WEH) Map, Smoothed Water Equivalent Hydrogen (WEH) Map, Thermal & Epithermal Neutron Counts Map, Iron Concentration Map, and Iron Gamma Counts Map. These maps have been generated from the GRaND L1B data taken during the Ceres Low Altitude Mapping Orbit phase of the Dawn mission.
